About the Shop

Onik's been your
mechanic for 32 years.

Burbank Auto Tech and Brake Repair is a family-run shop on S. Glenoaks Boulevard. Owner Onik has spent over three decades building a reputation on one principle: do the work right, charge a fair price, and treat every customer like a neighbor.

From brake overhauls and engine diagnostics to routine maintenance and motorcycle repair, Onik handles it all — and he'll always tell you exactly what your car needs and why.
